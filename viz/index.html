There's more to NCAA women's basketball than UConn

BY KARA NEWHOUSE | MAY 3, 2022

For most of the 2010s, the University of Connecticut dominated women's college basketball. The Huskies have won 11 NCAA championships — the most of any program — all with Coach Geno Auriemma at the helm.

When current WNBA superstar Breanna Stewart played for UConn from 2013-2016, the team won four consecutive titles. Back then, it wasn't hard to find someone asking whether UConn’s success was “bad for women’s basketball.” But it's been six years since UConn won a national title.
